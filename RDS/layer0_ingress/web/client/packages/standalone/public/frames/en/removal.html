Disable Sciebo RDS



Attention! You are about to disable Sciebo RDS!


What does this mean?

You will no longer be able to use RDS, access your projects or use the data management capabilities.

Will I still be able to access my Sciebo data, if I disable Sciebo RDS?

Your Sciebo account is seperate from your RDS account and your data will not be touched. You will still be able to use Sciebo as usual.

Can I reactivate my Sciebo RDS at a later time?

Yes, but your projects and data mangement plans will be gone.

Feedback

If you are sure you do not want to use Sciebo RDS anymore, we would appreciate your honest feedback. RDS is under constant development to satisfy all our users.

In case you want to keep using Sciebo RDS but need help using it, please contact support at bla@bla.de.
