Welcome to sciebo RDS!


Your sciebo extension for managing research data


sciebo Research Data Services (sciebo RDS) is a non-commercial service for research, studies and teaching and offers you the possibility to easily manage your research data.

Via sciebo RDS you can access your data located in the university cloud sciebo, bundle them in projects, add meta information and publish your data sets.


Get started in two easy steps:

  1. Connect a repository.
  2. Create a new project.

Find further information on our help page.
